Instructions

Welcome to Hogwarts!

The Sorting Hat will give you a random number.

To find out which house you are in, click on any of the house images to get a value. Continue clicking until you match with the Sorting Hat's number!

If you can manage to get a match with the Sorting Hat, a win will be added.

If you go over the Sorting Hat's number, a loss will be added.

The last image you click on is the house you will be sorted into, no matter if its a win or loss!

The game ends when you win or lose 4 times!

Have Fun!
